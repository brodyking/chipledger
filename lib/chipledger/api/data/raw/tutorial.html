Installing on iOS
To install Chipledger on a iOS device, follow these instructions in order:
  1. Press the share button () at the bottom of the page.
  2. Scroll down and click "Add to Home Screen "
  3. Once a new menu opens, press "Add" located at the top right.
If everything has worked correctly, it should appear on your homescreen!
Creating Games
To create a new game, navigate to the games page.

On mobile, you can do so by clicking the button at the top of the screen.

On desktop, simply press the Games link at the top of the page.

Then press New Game.

Enter your desired name and press Create.
